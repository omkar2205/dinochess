How to play

Chess rules, dinosaur movement

Every dinosaur follows the movement rules of its matching chess piece. Select a dinosaur to reveal its legal destinations.

  • T-Rex is the King.
  • Spinosaurus is the Queen.
  • Ankylosaurus is the Rook.
  • Pteranodon is the Bishop.
  • Velociraptor is the Knight.
  • Compsognathus is the Pawn.

Checkmate the opposing T-Rex to win.
