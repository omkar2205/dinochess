How to play

Chess rules, dinosaur pieces

Every dinosaur follows the movement rules of its matching chess piece. Select a piece to reveal its legal destinations.

  • Brachiosaurus is the King.
  • T-Rex is the Queen.
  • Triceratops is the Rook.
  • Pterodactyl is the Bishop.
  • Parasaurolophus is the Knight.
  • Dinosaur Egg is the Pawn.

Checkmate the opposing Brachiosaurus to win.
